Mwasalat Misr Bus Services

Cairo's Premium Bus Network

Mwasalat Misr is Cairo's modern bus transit system offering premium, air-conditioned bus services across Greater Cairo and connecting to major cities since 2017.

  • Over 30 routes across Greater Cairo
  • Modern air-conditioned fleet with WiFi
  • Real-time tracking via mobile application

Mwasalat Misr connects with Cairo metro lines at key stations and offers express services to New Administrative Capital
