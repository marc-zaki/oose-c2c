Light Rail Transit (LRT) Details

Cairo's Light Rail Transit System

The Light Rail Transit (LRT) is a 70km electrified rail system connecting Cairo to the New Administrative Capital and 10th of Ramadan City, with 12 stations since 2022.

  • 70km electrified rail network
  • 12 stations across the route
  • Connects Cairo to New Administrative Capital

The LRT connects with Cairo metro line 3 at Adly Mansour transportation hub
